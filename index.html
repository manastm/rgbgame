Impact Your Community

It's time to spread your passions to the community

No matter what your interests are, whether you love to play music, code videogames, cook international desserts, you can find a way to spread this passion to the members of your community.

Create or join an existing project to collaborate with other likeminded individuals to impact your community. Whether you join up with a group of musicians planning a flash mob at a local mall, or gather some programming enthusiasts to teach local kids to code, you can use your talents to make your community a better place.
